My experience in
customer relationship management:

As a bakery sales manager, our team is the strategic link between the production, catering and logistics departments and ~150+ wholesale accounts and customers.

While development is my main mission, account management and customer service are an essential part of my day-to-day as well. For an artisanal bakery specializing in sourdough bread and traditional pastries made from scratch and delivered every day of the year, keeping customers happy is no small feat.

Example

One example was when the pastry team forgot to add salt to the croissant dough, rendering it tasteless and inedible.

When I arrived the product had been delivered already. I immediately identified the accounts who received the product and prioritized key accounts based on sales volume, then created a script and divided priority accounts among our team. We called all accounts impacted within a few hours, adjusted invoices, sent emails to anyone we missed and coordinated the delivery of alternative product to those in desperate need.

The team went back over this mishap and put better systems in place to avoid something of this scale happening again.
